Hello, and welcome to my page! My name is Lubna. I am currently a student studying Computer Science at the University of Illinois at Chicago. I have been a photographer for almost ten years now. I have years of experience with film and digital photography, and I have experience with Photoshop-based digital imaging as well. Living most of my life through a creative lens, I've acquired a unique perspective that shows in my photography. My work all centers around beauty, elegance, and a little bit of fantasy. I am in love with life and all that the universe has to offer. My main goal in life is to embark on as many adventures as possible, and I love to share little pieces of my experiences with the world. Feel free to look through my pictures, thumb through my blog posts, and please feel free to contact me any time! I love to connect with new people, and it would be amazing to get to know you.
